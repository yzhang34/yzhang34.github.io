Biography

I am a postdoctoral scholar at the Caltech Optical Imaging Laboratory, Caltech, under the supervision of Prof. Lihong V. Wang. My research is interdisciplinary and focused on developing new types of optical imaging techniques that could advance the work of other researchers and medical personnel in a wide variety of fields. My research interests include photoacoustic microscopy, photoacoustic computed tomography, compressed ultrafast photography, nonlinear microscopy, multiphoton microscopy, fluorescence lifetime imaging microscopy, super-resolution microscopy, high-speed imaging, deep tissue imaging, adaptive optics, computational imaging, deep learning.
